Personality trait you should know about 'Corgi' is that their affectionate nature makes them quite needy, it can be seen as quite endearing by some owners but it may cause issues for other owners who have very busy lifestyles and can not devote so much time to giving their corgis attention. They are extremely sociable and hate to be left alone. They are at most risk of developing separation anxiety when you leave home.
